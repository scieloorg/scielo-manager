Parallell Titles

Enter parallel titles in accordance with the sequence and typography in which they appear on the title page or its substitute, according to ISBD(S) rules. In case of more than one parallel title, enter them in the sequence which appears on the title page, separated by ENTER (one by line).

Examples:

  1. Publication Title: Archives of toxicology / Parallel Title: Archiv fur Toxikologie
  2. Publication Title: Arzneimittel Forschung / Parallel Title: Drug research
